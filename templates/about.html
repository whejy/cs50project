About

Hello and welcome to My Journal!

My name is John and I am from Adelaide, Australia. I built this application in 2021 as part of my final project for CS50. My goal was to provide a pure experience as close as possible to simply writing on paper.

The advantages that My Journal has over a standard word processor are that your filenames and storage are handled automatically; your account is password protected and your entries are not stored on your local machine (useful for privacy if your computer is shared); and the application provides a clean snapshot of all your entries at once through your dashboard. At any point you are free to download to disk all of your entries and delete your account.

As I develop my skills, I may return to this project to add the ability to choose a colour theme.

Thank you for taking the time to check out My Journal!
